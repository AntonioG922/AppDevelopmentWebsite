    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do I need any app development or programming experience to join?

    No! Whether you're a certified expert in app development or just learning what programming is we'd love to have you join the club. Our members range from novice programmers taking their first classes to students with years of experience under their belts. While we would encourage you to take a look at the app development software and environment, we're more than happy to introduce you to the subject of app development!

    Do I need any programming experience to join?

    No

    When does the club meet?

    Wednesdays at 8:00 PM in the Student Involvement Room (located in Talley Student Union)

    Are there any club dues?

    No
